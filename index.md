We study comparative toxicology across species to understand chemical sensitivity, develop new approach methodologies, and improve environmental and human health risk assessment.

## Location

The Comparative Toxicology Lab is based at the [University of Birmingham](https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/) in the [School of Biosciences](https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/schools/biosciences) and the
[Centre for Environmental Research and Justice](https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/research/centre-environmental-research-justice)  


---

## Find Us

The Comparative Toxicology Lab is located in the Biosciences Building at the University of Birmingham's Edgbaston campus.
